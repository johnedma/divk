Who Cares?

Just last year more than 30 tech companies made a commitment to the White House to track and set workforce diversity goals. The pledge by companies to make their workforce more representative of the public was signed by a number of high-profile companies including
  • Airbnb, Box, GitHub, GoDaddy, Intel, Lyft, Medium, Pinterest, Spotify, Zynga and others.
